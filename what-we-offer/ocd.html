We are currently developing a workshop for parents who have OCD. This will be similar to the standard Confident Children Course but tailored to meet the specific needs of parents who have OCD.

We don’t have dates for the first course yet, but these are likely to be in late 2019. If you would like to take part, you will first need a referral to the Sussex OCD clinic.
