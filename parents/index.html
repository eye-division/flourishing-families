Parenting is hard at the best of times, add in mental health difficulties and it gets even tougher...

At the Flourishing Families Clinic we provide parenting support through the ‘Raising Confident Children’ courses, and we’ve also created this website to act as a resource for parent with mental health difficulties.

We hope you find the website a useful tool for information and tips on parenting in the context of mental health difficulties.
