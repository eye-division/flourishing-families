Parenting is hard at the best of times, but for parents with mental health difficulties parenting can be more challenging...

At the Flourishing Families Clinic we provide parenting support through our ‘Raising Confident Children’ courses.

As part of our commitment to supporting parents we’ve created this website as a resource for parents with mental health difficulties. We hope the website provides a useful tool, providing  information and tips on parenting in the context of mental health difficulties.
